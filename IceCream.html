ABOUT US

VELLE ICECREAMS is one of the Gorakhpur's oldest ice-cream parlours. Vellle Icecreams has been pioneer in making artisan ice creams using only fruits, dry fruits, chocolates, milk and sugar and continues to hold its place, despite the entry of dozens of other ice-cream parlours. We add no preservatives in our products. It’s been an ideal stop over for ice creams from past several years.
People still drive up to Velle Icecreams after dinner to pick up ice creams... …a tradition that has continued for years now. The ice-cream parlour has seen a lot of changes in terms of its decorum and luxurious ambiance. We are very well lit up and the decorum is extremely classy and elegant, perfect for a family meal.
Yummiest and mouth watering products like sugar-free, eggless cakes have been introduced. We have recently introduced awesomest and unique Bhel Puri Sundae, Cheery Berry Sundae, and in scoops and cones as well, like the Malai Kulfi, Death by Chocolate and Dairy Milk Chocolate. You can Also try yummy and delicious Ice Cream Cakes, which come in four flavours.
